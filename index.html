Interview!

  • High Concept: Get the man to his job interview before the time runs out, while dodging obstacles.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they don't get too bored with my game. The players would learn to love colors!
  • Genre: This game fits into a kids genre, because it's colorful and cartoonish for children.
  • Game play:
  • 1. Wait for everything to load.
  • 2. Get the man to the other side of town.
  • 3. Avoid obstacles by moving the cursor.
  • Prototype goal: Get to the interview before the time runs out.
  • Features: Cool music and very colorful.
  • Setting/Premise:
    • This game is abstract. Made in a make believe place located in a town with few little people. The man is running late for his interview. He has to get there before his timeslot is over. There is no backstory and there are no tokens.
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: This game is targeted for children 5 years old and under.
  • Play time: The game is finished in like 10 - 20 seconds.
  • Strategies: The player should first try to avoid the obstacles. Be quick about it before the time runs out. You'll die if you touch them, or at least I want it to happen, that's still a work in progress.
